Services Page

Our Range of Security Services

K1 Protection provides professional security services for businesses, organisations, venues and private clients. We offer flexible security solutions based on your requirements.

Our services are designed to protect people, property and businesses while maintaining a safe and professional environment.

Door Supervisors

Our door supervisors provide a professional front-line security presence for licensed premises, events, hotels, restaurants and private functions. They assist with entry control, customer safety, conflict management, incident prevention and maintaining a safe environment for staff, customers and visitors.

CCTV Operators

We provide CCTV operators trained to monitor surveillance systems, identify risks, report suspicious activity and support incident response. CCTV plays an important role in protecting premises, preventing crime and supporting safety.

Security Guards

Our security guards protect commercial premises, construction sites, warehouses, offices, retail locations and residential developments. They provide visible deterrence, patrols, access control, visitor management and incident reporting.

Close Protection

K1 Protection offers close protection services for individuals who require personal safety, privacy and professional security support. Our approach is discreet, organised and focused on risk management.

Event Security

We support events with trained security staff who can assist with crowd control, access management, visitor safety, emergency response and general event protection.

Construction Site Security

We provide security guards for construction sites to help prevent theft, trespassing, vandalism and unauthorised access. Our officers support access control, patrols, site monitoring and incident reporting.

Retail Security

Our retail security services help reduce theft, support staff safety and provide a professional security presence for shops, shopping centres and commercial premises.

Mobile Patrols

K1 Protection can provide mobile patrol services for businesses, vacant properties, construction sites and commercial premises. Mobile patrols help deter crime and check site security.

Key Holding & Alarm Response

We can support clients with key holding and alarm response services where required. This helps businesses respond safely to alarm activations without placing staff or property owners at unnecessary risk.
